Who we are

We need to track our time every day. We are you.

It took over 15 years in software development before the idea of creating Spent time came to mind. Those were years of time consuming planning, tracking and progress reporting on our spent time in addition to being busy with the actual work at hand. (It was normal to log time for logging time).

During this time many different systems were proposed, hyped, used, silently died and forgotten about until the next system to 'manage it all' came along. Time and again the cheering about the new system would go hand in hand with finding a way to manage estimated, planned and actual hours. Most of the time it turned out that time tracking was taking a back seat and you would end up fiddling around with excel, your mailbox or some half-baked time registration solution.

That had to change we decided and designed a real-time system which would have time tracking at it's core. We enabled it to connect to the many systems out there for project management, issue tracking and finance. The systems around you change, Spent time stays.

Give it a spin for as long as you want with a forever free account and let us know what you think or ask any questions trough the in-app feedback, send us an email at support@spent-time.com
Your feedback is most valuable to us and we are waiting to answer any and all of your questions.

Hope to see you around some time soon!

Martijn Benjamin
Founder | Spent-time.com

What's in the box?

Spent time provides you with the tools to

STAY ON TRACK

The configurable dashboard will keep you informed on your spent time and tells you if you are still on track for today, this week and this month.

TIME EFFECTIVELY

The time tracker makes planning and tracking very easy and drastically reduces your time spent tracking your spent time.

PIN WORK DOWN

The item tracker makes it easy to organize and configure your tasks at hand. Pair up your spent time with your work items in no time.

CONNECT SERVICES

With a few clicks you can connect to a wide range of online services like Trello, Github or Quickbooks and let Spent time keep it all in sync.

REPORT ON TIME

Either use the included templates or roll your own reports and invoices to create your PDF or an export file in the format of choice.

REMEMBER

With notifications you can be reminded of things that need doing, how long you have been at it or be alerted on exceeding limits.
